Where will the bead land?

Assume there is no air resistance and the table is frictionless after the bead leaves the bowl. Which statement is true immediately after the bead leaves the bowl?

  • A. It stops because it has reached the bottom.
  • B. It keeps moving horizontally at constant speed.
  • C. It accelerates upward because the bowl was curved.
  • D. It reverses direction and climbs back up the bowl.
